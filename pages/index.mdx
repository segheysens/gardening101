# Welcome to Gardening 101!

Gardening 101 is an open source public resource for gardening information. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced gardener, you'll find valuable tips and insights to help you grow and maintain your garden.

## What is Gardening 101?

Gardening 101 is a **comprehensive** and **user-friendly** platform that provides a wealth of gardening knowledge. From plant care guides to troubleshooting common issues, Gardening 101 has everything you need to become a successful gardener.

## Documentation

For detailed instructions and tutorials, please visit our documentation at [https://gardening101.org](https://gardening101.org).
